About Airplane Conflict Detector

Conflict detection and resolution has a long history of investigation, but further investigation is necessary as a result of new Free Flight policies removing many constraints; new data link, GPS, and avionics technologies; and an increased emphasis on economic and market factors. In this paper, we analyze Free Flight conflict detection and resolution problems and solve two-aircraft encounter problems with tactical/strategic cases.

WHAT ACD COME FROM?

ACD is the open-source python program which can detect the airplane which are conflict each other. Do you know that in the lightest day for air traffic was the start of the new year, Jan. 1, 2017, when — at the day's peak — there were 3,354 planes in the sky at the same time. Just human cannot track every airplane carefully. So, we have to help them to detect the conflict.
